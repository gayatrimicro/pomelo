Update patient information anywhere, anytime

Free-up valuable time and resources by digitizing forms that patients can complete in the comfort of their own homes, with their own devices.Have patients complete context specific e-forms, which automatically populate their chart and update within the provider’s EMR.

Customizable Patient Forms

Time & Response Based Triggers
E-forms can be automated to go out to patients before or after appointment booking, during check-in, or after their appointment. Forms and questionnaires can also be designed to be responsive to patients’ answers.
‍
Forms by Appointment-Type
Save time and keep organized by attaching specific forms to the right appointment type. For example, providers can automatically send a form to a patient as soon as they check-in to their appointment, so they may fill it out while they wait.
‍
Medical Form Creation
Providers also get access to a collection of popular forms that can be copied or modified. Clinics can even create their own forms, or request the Pomelo team to develop custom forms.

EMR Integration

Integration Preferences
Form responses can be pushed to the patients’ charts automatically upon their completion, or they can be reviewed by clinic staff/providers beforehand; this can be customized for each e-form.

Allow patients to share health information via secure forms and questionnaires.
